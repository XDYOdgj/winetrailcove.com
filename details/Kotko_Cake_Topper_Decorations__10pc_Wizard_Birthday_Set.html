Magical Wizard Cake Toppers – The Perfect Charm for Any Celebration

Add a touch of fantasy and fun to your wizard-themed party with these Kotko cake toppers. Designed for both boys and girls, this vibrant set features enchanting designs like a towering castle, a whimsical witch hat, and festive 'Happy Birthday' signs that will captivate every guest and create unforgettable memories.

Each topper is made from high-quality bend-resistant cardstock, ensuring it stays sturdy and shape intact even when placed on a cake.
